Our Story

AURA11 Studios is Hyderabad’s premier destination for cinematic storytelling and modern visual production.

Aura11 Studios has been founded on one thought and that thought is to say that every moment has the right to be expressed in a beautiful manner. Where the initial passion started to freeze the frame of the moment and blend into a visual that has to be not only seen, but also felt and has an influence, we realized the gap that existed within the current scenario of content being overflowed within the society and that had to be changed.

At Aura11 Studios, we aim to not only capture images, but to extract the storytelling of every brand, every event, and every individual that comes to us. Our projects, big and small, receive the same passion and artistic integrity, thanks to our professional cameras, lighting, aerial views, and data-intensive post-production segment.

We listen to you—We map out the next steps—We work together—We hand over the result — no delays, with the same high quality, including full production, conceptualization to shooting,and right down to high-quality VFX, color, and audio with the same level of care.
